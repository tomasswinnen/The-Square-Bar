Box Dimensions (Interior)

  • Width: 144mm (2 × 70mm + 4mm)
  • Length: 106mm (6 × 17mm + 4mm)
  • Height: 74mm (70mm + 4mm)

  • Contents: 12 bars, 2 col × 6 rows
  • Mix: 6 Protten + 6 Datten

Panel Dimensions (mm)

  • Front / Back: 144 × 74
  • Side A / Side B: 106 × 74
  • Glue flap: 15 × 74
  • Tuck flaps (×2): 144 × 40
  • Tuck tab: 120w × 6d, r=4
  • Dust flaps (×4): 45 × 40
  • Total dieline: 515 × 154mm

Assembly Sequence

  • 1. Score all fold lines (blue dashed)
  • 2. Apply adhesive to GLUE flap
  • 3. Fold Side A → Back → Side B
  • 4. Glue flap adheres to Front interior
  • 5. Load 12 bars (2 col × 6 rows, upright)
  • 6. Fold dust flaps inward at both ends
  • 7. Insert tuck tabs to lock both ends
