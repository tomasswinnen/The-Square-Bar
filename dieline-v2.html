Box Dimensions (Interior)

  • Width: 144mm (2 bars × 70mm + 4mm)
  • Length: 106mm (6 bars × 17mm + 4mm)
  • Height: 74mm (bar height 70mm + 4mm)
  • Cardboard thickness: 2mm
  • Contents: 12 bars — 2 col × 6 rows, upright
  • Mix: 6 Protten + 6 Datten

Panel Dimensions

  • Front / Back: 144 × 74mm
  • Side A / Side B: 106 × 74mm
  • Glue flap: 15 × 74mm
  • Top + Bottom tuck flaps: 144 × 40mm
  • Tuck tab: 120mm wide, 6mm deep, r=4mm
  • Dust flaps (×4): 45 × 40mm
  • Total dieline: 515 × 154mm

Assembly Instructions

  • 1. Score all fold lines (blue dashed)
  • 2. Apply adhesive to glue flap (hatch area)
  • 3. Fold along Side A–Front join
  • 4. Fold along Back–Side A join
  • 5. Fold Side B around and glue to Front interior
  • 6. Load bars: 2 columns × 6 rows, standing upright
  • 7. Fold dust flaps inward at both openings
  • 8. Insert tuck flaps to lock top + bottom
