The Square Bar — 12-Pack Box Dieline Specification

Box Dimensions (Interior)

  • Width: 144mm (2 bars × 70mm + 4mm tolerance)
  • Length: 424mm (6 bars × 70mm + 4mm tolerance)
  • Height: 74mm (bar height 70mm + 4mm)
  • Cardboard: 2mm thickness
  • Bars: 12 total — 2 columns × 6 rows, standing upright

Panel Dimensions (mm)

  • Front / Back: 144 × 74
  • Side A / Side B: 424 × 74
  • Glue flap: 15 × 74
  • Top + Bottom tuck flaps: 144 × 40 (with 106mm tab, r=6mm)
  • Dust flaps (×4): 60 × 40
  • Dieline total: 1151 × 154mm

Layer Legend

CUT — outer cut edge
FOLD — score lines
SAFE ZONE — 5mm inset
BLEED — 3mm outset

Assembly Instructions

  • 1. Score all blue dashed fold lines
  • 2. Apply glue to GLUE flap (hatch area)
  • 3. Fold Side A and Side B inward
  • 4. Glue flap adheres to inner edge of Front
  • 5. Fold dust flaps inward first at top/bottom
  • 6. Insert tuck tabs to close top and bottom
  • 7. Fill with 12 bars (2 col × 6 rows, upright)
